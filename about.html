About the club

Cricket first, community always — building a West Indian–friendly gathering place in the Poconos.

Mission

To create a consistent cricket community in the Mt Pocono area where adults (20+) can play recreationally, organize competitive matches, and build lasting connections.

Who it’s for

Adults who want to play, learn, or get back into cricket. All skill levels welcome—new players included. The club aims to bring West Indian culture and community together through sport.

What to expect

Structured practices, a fair-play culture, and match opportunities as the roster grows. We’ll publish official rules, dues (if any), and schedule as we finalize logistics.

Club values

Simple standards that keep the club strong.

  • Respect: for teammates, opponents, and the field.
  • Inclusion: welcoming environment for newcomers and returning players.
  • Consistency: reliable updates and predictable sessions once scheduled.
  • Community: cricket as a bridge to friendship and culture.
