About Pocono Cricket Club

A community-first cricket club for adults (20+) in the Mt Pocono area—recreational play with room to grow into competitive matches.

Our purpose

Cricket is the connector. Community is the point.

Pocono Cricket Club is being built for people who want regular cricket, good vibes, and a consistent group. We’re especially focused on bringing West Indian cricket culture and community together in the Poconos.

What we’re building

  • Recreational sessions: organized practice and friendly games for all skill levels.
  • Competitive fixtures: as membership grows, we’ll schedule matches with visiting teams.
  • Community events: meetups that help people connect beyond the pitch.

Timeline

  • First meeting: Spring 2026
  • Practice & schedule: TBD (published as soon as field details are confirmed)
  • Area: Mt Pocono + surrounding counties

How to join

Register once—get updates as we finalize details.

  1. Submit the interest form.
  2. We’ll email you practice and meeting details.
  3. Show up, meet the group, and play.
